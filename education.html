My Education



This is a quick overview of my education from childhood to the current time. I started school at the age of 5 until 6 years old at a kindergarten called 'Tadika Islam Sinaran Ilmu'



When I'm 7 years old, I entered the same school as my older brothers which are 'Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Putra Perdana' (SKTPP) in morning and 'Sri Al-Hatem KAFA' in evening.



I have to switch schools at the age of eight because SKTPP is already at capacity. Since the new school was built behind my house, my parents no longer have to drive me to school every day, which is a benefit for them. I can get to school by walking alone. The name of my new school was 'Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Putra Perdana 2' (SKTPP2). In addition, since Sri Al-Hatem KAFA did not provide a PSRA test, I also transferred to a newly built religious school when I was nine years old. It's name was 'Sekolah Rendah Agama Putra Perdana' (SRAPP).



Then, I started going to high school which is 'Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Putra Perdana' (SMKPP). But, I changed schools after one week of study there because I got a boarding school at Sabak Bernam. It is a religious school and its name was 'Sekolah Agama Menengah Parit Baru' (SAMPB). Religious schools are my choice since they prioritize the bond between males and girls. In form 4, I chose major in accounting and economics.



Lastly, I decided to study Information Management (IM110) at 'Universiti Teknologi MARA' (UITM), Rembau. I have five semesters to complete my diploma. This is the last semester of my diploma.
